— Our Story

More Than a Café, It's a Community

Started in 2012 by alumni who wanted a better campus coffee experience, Brew & Study has grown into the beating heart of college life. Whether you're cramming for exams, catching up with friends, or just need a quiet corner — we've got you.

We source our beans from small farms in Coorg and Chikmagalur, roast them weekly, and craft every cup with intention. Our kitchen team prepares everything fresh daily — no frozen shortcuts here.

📶High-speed Wi-Fi across all seating zones
🔌Charging points at every table
📚Quiet study pods for focused work
🌿Vegan & allergy-friendly options available
